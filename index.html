About our pizza

What does make great pizza great? Crispy dough, made out of the gold flour imported right from the Italian mills? Fresh vegetables and greenery gathered this morning from our personal garden? Or maybe secret-recipe sauces exclusively created for our pizzas to emphasize their unique taste? Is it love and soul that are fluttering every slice? It is all of these combined in one. All our pizza is about the best-made dough, natural ingredients, special sauces created individually for every pizza type and our love.

Why our pizza?

Love is the main ingredient for every bite of our pizza. All famous Italian chiefs had a hand in creating our own absolutely unique menu. Pizza combinations were created by months, while the absolute taste was not achieved. Unique toppings in the whole country can reverse your images about pizza to 180 degrees. Even complete pizza-haters will change their minds after tasting just one slice.

Our delivery

For years we have been working on our delivery system. Every month more workers and machines are implemented into it. Franchises are distributed throughout the whole country proportionally, so time a delivery guy is wasting on road is no more than 7 minutes. New kitchen technologies allow to make pizza faster, more comfortable and in bigger amounts with no harm to taste, which minimizes the delivery time as well. The maximum time to deliver our pizza is 40 minutes. We promise, that if you have to wait even one additional minute, your order will be for free.
